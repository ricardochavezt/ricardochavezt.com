Some notes on the latest Pokémon Presents (which I totally did not stumble upon by random chance on YouTube when looking for a video to watch while having lunch 😆):

- in case it’s not clear from the previous post 😆, I think that gadget that looks like an original GameBoy and plays the music from the original Red and Blue versions is kinda unnecessary and a play on nostalgia. Personally, I don’t see any reason at all to buy it when you can listen to remix versions or [beautiful symphonic arrangements](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=OLAK5uy_msSximDxthAunzBUbDvDXFssBdLSzN6dc&si=CC83__jJrJAtUKx2) or [piano renditions](https://youtu.be/9XsqwdGE5z0?si=LCeS4KSJe6AZo8sB)… but that’s just me 😄.
- I like the names of the next generation games: Pokémon Winds and Pokémon Waves. Can’t believe they haven’t been used till now.
- The event in San Francisco with the pop-up Pokémon Center looks nifty, but I don’t know if it’d be better to go there or save for a trip to Japan and go to the permanent Pokémon Centers (and then take the chance and visit so many other things while there)
- I didn’t care much about the other things 😅. Just leave me with my fangames and I’ll be happy 😄.  
(Pokopia looks cute tho)
